GOPchop Development Online Discussion

Mailing Lists

The GOPchop mailing list is the primary place to discuss GOPchop, ask questions, meet other users, share patches, etc.

IRC

#gopchop on server irc.freenode.org. People can usually help with quick compile or operational issues.

Configure your IRC client to use the utf-8 character set if you can. In many IRC clients, this can be done with ‘/charset utf-8’ or ‘/set term_type utf-8’.
